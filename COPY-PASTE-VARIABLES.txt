2. DB_PORT=${{Postgres.PGPORT}}

4. DB_PASSWORD=${{Postgres.PGPASSWORD}}
5. DB_DATABASE=${{Postgres.PGDATABASE}}
6. JWT_SECRET=GxeqiBSQjmoD6JnkKHuA347sbZrh5VylvgL1XOdP
7. JWT_EXPIRATION=7d
8. NODE_ENV=production
9. PORT=3000

===== IMPORTANT =====
Replace "Postgres" with your actual PostgreSQL service name in Railway!

===== HOW TO ADD =====
1. Railway Dashboard → Backend service → Variables tab
2. Click "+ New Variable"
3. For each variable:
   - Key: DB_HOST (copy from above)
   - Value: ${{Postgres.PGHOST}} (copy from above, replace Postgres with your service name)
   - Click "Add"
4. Repeat for all 10 variables
